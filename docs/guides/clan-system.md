!!! note
Clans allow players to work together to control large territories. For information about territory calculations, see the [Territory System](territory-system.md) section.
!!!

## Joining a Clan

!!! tip
The clan invitation link also works as a referral — when joining through a clan link, you become both a clan member and a referral of the player who invited you.
!!!

### Methods

#### Via Invitation Link
- Get a link from a clan member.
- Open the link and accept the invitation.

#### Direct Joining
- Find a clan-controlled area on the map.
- Press **Join** in the clan details.

!!! caution "Important"
When you join a clan, all your hexes automatically become part of its territory.
!!!

## Clan Chat

!!! tip "Important Advice"
**Be sure to create a Telegram chat for your clan!** This will dramatically improve coordination and effectiveness of team play.
!!!

### How to Add a Chat
1. Create a Telegram group for the clan.
2. The clan leader adds the chat link in the clan settings.

## Leaving a Clan

!!! caution
When leaving, your hexes are removed from clan territory. If you are the last member, the clan will be deleted.
!!!

Your hexes and status after leaving:
- Hexes become independent.
- You can immediately join a new clan, and all your hexes will transfer to that clan's territory.

## Clan Management

- The clan leader can edit the clan name, description, chat link, and remove members.
- Only the leader can delete the clan.
- Clans compete in leaderboards based on the territory they control.
