## Invitation Methods

Settlers has two ways to invite a player and make them your referral:

### 1. Clan Invitation
- Use the **Invite** button on the clan page.
- The player joins the clan and automatically becomes your referral.

### 2. Friend Invitation
- Use the **Invite friend** link from your profile.
- The player registers as your referral.

## Referrals

- You can see how many players have joined using your referral link.
- Referrals strengthen the community and help grow your clan.
- Future reward mechanics for referrals may be added as the game evolves.
